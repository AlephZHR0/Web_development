My top 3 series of all-time

Doctor Who

The series depicts the adventures of the Doctor, an alien from the fictional species of Time Lords from the planet Gallifrey, who explores the universe in his machine capable of traveling through space and time, known as the TARDIS (Time and Relative Dimension in Space), which externally resembles a police box...

Jujutsu Kaisen

The young Yuta Okkotsu gains control of an extremely powerful spirit, and a group of sorcerers enroll him in the Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School, to help him control this power and also to keep an eye on him.

The Seven Deadly Sins

The 'Seven Deadly Sins' was the strongest group of knights defending the region of Britannia. Ten years ago, they were accused of attempting a coup to take over the kingdom and were supposedly thwarted by the Paladins, immensely powerful holy warriors from the kingdom of Lyonesse, and fled.
